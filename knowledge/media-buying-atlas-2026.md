**Part II: Meta Ads and the Andromeda Revolution**

**2.1 How Andromeda Actually Works**

Andromeda is not an optimization feature or a dashboard update. It is a foundational, systemic overhaul of how ad retrieval functions. Built on deep neural networks utilizing NVIDIA Grace Hopper Superchips, the system introduces a 10,000x increase in model computational complexity at the pre-auction stage.

The operational logic has inverted. The old system asked: 'Who should I show this specific ad to?' The new system asks: 'Which version of an ad should I show this specific user at this exact micro-moment?' This inversion has rendered traditional media buying tactics such as detailed interest targeting and micro-optimizations entirely obsolete.

**The Andromeda Retrieval Pipeline**

- Step 1: Computer vision scans every creative in real-time using Entity IDs (actual visual pixels, hook pacing, emotional tone, text overlays, psychological signals)

- Step 2: Matches creative signals to a user's latent intent and psychological state across 3+ billion people

- Step 3: Pulls approximately 1,000 candidates from the retrieval pool

- Step 4: ARM (Adaptive Ranking Model) ranks candidates based on clean conversion data quality

- Step 5: Winners enter the standard ad auction

**2.3 Entity Clustering: The Most Critical Concept in 2026 Media Buying**

Entity Clustering is the mechanism by which Meta's computer vision and semantic analysis models group visually and contextually similar ads into a single entity. Under Andromeda, if an advertiser uploads 50 ads featuring the same image with minor adjustments (headline, primary text, CTA button color), the algorithm treats all 50 variations as a single retrieval ticket.

Creative diversity is no longer about tweaking a hook. It requires entirely different formats, distinct consumer personas, and radically different messaging angles. The algorithm requires unique inputs across four core creative levers:

- **Persona:** Different faces, ages, ethnicities, environments (e.g., 28yo urban mom vs 45yo suburban dad)

- **Messaging:** Different pain points, benefits, proof mechanisms, emotional angles

- **Hook:** Different opening frameworks (problem-aware, benefit-led, social proof, curiosity, comparison)

- **Format:** UGC, podcast reads, static images, founder stories, carousel, meme, VSL, claymation, AI avatars

**Post-click impact:** Traffic directed to broad category pages saw a devastating 24% collapse in ROAS, while traffic directed to specific product pages saw consistent gains. Because Andromeda delivers colder top-of-funnel traffic based on predictive modeling, users require immediate decision-making clarity.

**Part V: Data, Attribution, and CAPI: The Signal Layer**

**5.1 The CAPI Imperative**

Browser Pixel alone is now 'garbage in.' iOS restrictions, ad blockers, and express checkouts kill 30-60% of signals. CAPI (Conversions API) with high Event Match Quality (EMQ of 8+) is non-negotiable. It feeds the algorithm real Purchase/ViewContent/ATC events tied to email, phone, and click ID so Andromeda's ARM trusts and prioritizes your ads.

**Exact Meta CAPI + Pixel Setup (\$100K+/Month Standard)**

- **Server-side first, Pixel as backup.** Use official Meta CAPI or partners like Weld, CustomerLabs, wetracked.io, Stape.

- **Mandatory parameters for EMQ 8-10:** external_id (hashed email/phone), em (email), ph (phone), fbp/fbc (from cookie), ct (client IP), user_agent.

- **Deduplication:** Always pass event_id + action_source = 'website' so Meta matches browser + server events without double-counting.

**The 5-Minute EMQ Audit**

21. Open Meta Event Debugger on your top 3 winning creatives

22. Check every Purchase event: must have external_id, em, ph, fbc/fbp, event_id for dedup

23. If EMQ is below 7 on more than 20% of events, your ARM is crippled

24. Fix: Stape or Weld server-side container + Shopify webhook to auto-fire with all params

**Result reported in community:** 15-40% CPM drop and ROAS stabilization within 48 hours on \$100K+/month accounts.
